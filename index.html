About

Hello, my name is Jovana Damjanović. I am born in Montenegro in 2006. Since the age of 5 people around me noticed my passion for art and design. At that moment my artistic life began. I have participated in over 50 art competitions, of which I won 3 first places for the most creative work, 2 first places in fashion design competitions, 8 second places in art competitions and 3 third places in interior design. I have completed a 3D art design course, an interior design course and am a self-taught artist.
